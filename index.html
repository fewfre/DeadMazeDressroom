Dead Maze Dressroom (unfinished)

This is a free fan-made tool to allow people to create the perfect look for their character to share with friends without needing to own the item.

This dressroom was made for use by the Dead Maze Wiki in retrieving images.
However, it has been expanded to be a full-fledged easy to use dressroom.
Please enjoy!
